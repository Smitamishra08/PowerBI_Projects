# PowerBI_Projects Portfolio

This portfolio showcases end-to-end Power BI projects across healthcare, retail, and food service domains. It highlights data modeling, DAX, interactive dashboard design, and business insights derived from real-world datasets.
- Purpose: Demonstrate practical analytics and storytelling using Power BI.
- Audience: Recruiters, hiring managers, and collaborators.
- Contents: PBIX files, exported PDFs, and sample datasets.


Highlights across projects
- Cross-industry analytics: Healthcare costs and utilization, retail sales performance, and food service demand patterns.
- Data modeling & DAX: Relationships, calculated columns, measures, KPIs, and time intelligence.
- Interactive design: Slicers, drill-through, tooltips, bookmarks, and clean UX.
- Actionable insights: Revenue drivers, customer behavior, peak times, and optimization opportunities.


Project descriptions

Healthcare dashboard
- Objective: Analyze Medicare dataset to surface utilization, cost drivers, and patient demographics.
- Key insights: Top procedures by volume and cost, age group distribution, and provider performance trends.
- Skills demonstrated: Data cleaning, relationships, advanced DAX, KPIs, and healthcare-focused storytelling.
- Files: Medicare Dataset.xlsx, HealthcareDashboard.pbix, HealthcareDashboardPowerBI.pdf


Superstore sales dashboard
- Objective: Track sales, profit, and discount dynamics across regions, categories, and segments.
- Key insights: Profitability by category/subcategory, regional performance, discount vs. margin effects.
- Skills demonstrated: Data modeling, drill-through, interactive filters, revenue trend analysis.
- Files: Super Store Sales Dashboard.pdf


Pizza sales dashboard
- Objective: Optimize menu and operations by analyzing item mix, demand timing, and outlet contribution.
- Key insights: Best-selling pizzas, peak times (hour/day), outlet performance, and repeat customer behavior.
- Skills demonstrated: Time-series analysis, segmentation, DAX measures for retention and contribution, retail UX.
- Files: Pizza Sales Dashboard.pdf


Tools, skills, and setup
Tools and technologies
- Power BI Desktop: Development and visualization.
- DAX: Measures, KPIs, time intelligence, and calculations.
- Excel: Data preprocessing and lightweight transformations.
- PDF exports: Quick review of dashboards.

  
Skills demonstrated
- Data cleaning: Handling missing values and normalization.
- Modeling: Relationships, star schemas, and calculated columns.
- DAX measures: KPIs, percentages, YTD/MTD, moving averages.
- Interactivity: Slicers, drill-through, tooltips, and bookmarks.
- Design: Layout, color consistency, accessibility, and storytelling.
